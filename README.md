## Common Questions

**How do I manage or cancel my subscription?**
Open BeeClean → Settings → Manage Subscription, or go to your device Settings → your name → Subscriptions.

**How do I restore purchases?**
BeeClean → Settings → Manage Subscription → Restore Purchases.

**Is my data private?**
Photo analysis happens on your device. See our privacy policy inside the app: Settings → Privacy Policy.

**How do I delete my account?**
BeeClean → Settings → Delete Account.
